Features

Everything you need to
run your business

A complete point-of-sale suite built for the pace of real restaurants and shops.

Table Management

Visualize your floor plan, track table status in real time, split bills across guests, and merge orders with a single tap.

Lightning Fast Billing

Generate GST-compliant bills, thermal print receipts, and share them via WhatsApp in under 3 seconds. No slowdowns, ever.

UPI & Multi-payment

Accept UPI, card, cash, and split payments. Auto-generate QR codes per transaction. Reconcile at day-end in seconds.

Real-time Analytics

Live dashboards show top dishes, peak hours, revenue trends, and staff performance. Make data-driven decisions daily.

Smart Inventory

Track stock levels, set low-stock alerts, log wastage, and auto-deduct ingredients when dishes are sold. Zero manual effort.

Staff & Shifts

Add staff roles, manage shift timing, track individual sales performance, and set permission levels to protect sensitive data.

Works Offline

No internet? No problem. Serve Sync continues billing without a hitch and syncs all data the moment you're back online.

Mobile Waiter App

Waitstaff take orders directly from the table on their phones. Orders flow to the kitchen display instantly — no shouting required.
